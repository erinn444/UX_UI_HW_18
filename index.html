MY UX SKILLS

User Research

I use research at the foundation of my designs. The more I can empathize with users during the research process, the better the solution for the product. I conduct research through surveys, interviews, competitor analyses, and affinity diagrams.

Wireframing

I take the data I've gathered from user research and interviews and apply those insights into creating the initial design for a product. During this process, I like to start from a simple sketch and move to higher fidelity as I test and make iterations.

Prototyping

I use protoyping to bring the designs of the wireframes to life. This provides users with a functional design to test and allows for more authentic feedback on iterations to make for final designs.
